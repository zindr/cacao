Methodology: What is the best way to collect your data ? Does it guarantee that you get the answers you are looking for ?

Data Analysis

Years of experience in a wide variety of statistical techniques, and scientific description and interpretion of results, can help ensure appropriate statistical reasoning, visualisation, and offers fully documented and reproducible code in R.

How to perform the analyses ? Can the data be used as such, or does it require further data management ? How to best deal with the results ?

Consultancy and Data Analysis

Consultancy and data analysis is now the focus of my work. I have gained experience both as a freelancer and as a post-doc researcher who offered consultancy for both co-workers and third parties. References shows publications in international journals.

Independent

As a freelancer I have contributed in various types of research, both providing advice and running the analyses. This for example included the analysis of observed workload in nursing, the programming of automated test item analysis, running bootstrap experiments, item selection for test construction, shiny-app development and more. Clients came from the university, the governement and the industry.

University

As a statistical consultant at university I have dealt with various types of research, experimental and observational, in the fields of medicine, pharmacy, psychology, educational research, industry. While at first consultancy was mainly external, serving outside clients, gradually the emphasis turned to internal consultancy, serving researchers from my own research community.
Examples include analysis of pain over time, meta-analysis of the effectiveness of anesthetics, comparing the appreciation of different products, validation of scales, evaluating inter-rater reliability, analyzing changes in test results, and many more.

Research

I was a researcher in various research centers at university, dealing with different types of data (experimental, observational, naturalistic - cross-sectional, longitudinal) and many different types of models. References shows publications in international journals and talks at international congresses.

Methodology

With simulation and bootstrap experiments I have focused on sample size issues for multilevel and single case design within a meta-analysis approach.

Psychometrics

Using IRT-family models I have studied how to evaluate the learning of second language and mathematics test data. The aim was to automate the process in order to develop an adaptive learning environment.

Quantitative Psychology

The evolution of emotions over time and its interindividual variability I have considered using a dynamical systems approach on longitudinally observed emotion state data.

Experimental Psychology

Experiments were used to study social phenomenon like discrimination and perceptual phenomenon like the importance of novelty.

Sociology

The experienced effect of mergers in high-schools I studied quantitatively using survey research.
